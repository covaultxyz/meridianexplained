Your Platform, Your Way

MERIDIAN is modular. Everyone gets the engine. Then you activate the AI agents you actually need. Think of it like a phone plan — pick your features, scale when you're ready.

01
Tier 1
The Foundation

Everything runs on this. Your own AI infrastructure.

This is the base platform — the engine that makes everything else possible. Your own knowledge graph, your own messaging backbone, your own secure network. Your data stays in your instance. You own it.

Knowledge Graph

Your own persistent memory — searchable, scored, and connected. Every fact, every decision, every contact.

Encrypted Messaging

Private backbone for all agent communication. Everything moves through encrypted channels.

Built-In Redundancy

Minimum 2 nodes — one local, one cloud. If one goes down, the other keeps running.

Health & Security

Self-healing services, circuit breakers, rate limiting, dead letter queue, and full audit logging.

What's not included: Any AI agent capabilities. The base platform stores, routes, monitors, and secures — it doesn't think, analyze, or act until you add agent packages below.
