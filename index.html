Architecture That Sets Us Apart

01

Persistent Memory Graph (MIG)

Not a chat history — a living knowledge graph. 7,000+ memory nodes, 5,135 concepts with vector embeddings, all connected by 91,000+ typed relationships in Neo4j. Every fact has provenance, confidence scoring, temporal decay, and corroboration tracking. Memories don't just exist — they age, get verified, and earn promotion through a 3-tier gate system. No other AI system treats institutional knowledge as a first-class citizen with its own lifecycle.

02

Multi-Daemon Mesh Architecture

Not one AI assistant — a coordinated team of 8+ specialized AI daemons, each with a distinct role: Sensei (coherence & quality), Atlas (strategic intelligence), Delphi (infrastructure & oracle), Sandman (operations), Forge (compute), and more. They communicate via NATS messaging with identity lock, failover in under 30 seconds, and typed broadcast coordination. This is an AI org chart that runs itself.

03

Oracle Call Intelligence

Voice calls go in. Deal-qualified intelligence comes out. Calls are diarized (who said what), transcribed natively on Apple Silicon via MLX Whisper (no cloud dependency), then run through signal extraction, talk-track analysis, and coaching recommendations. Every call produces Contact nodes, Action Items, and deal-stage classification — automatically written into the knowledge graph. No manual CRM entry. No lost context.

04

Zero-Downtime Deployment Across 3 Nodes

Blue/green deploys to a 3-server mesh in under 20 minutes. Canary-first rollout with instant rollback — no rebuild required. 169 commits deployed in a single push without a second of downtime. Most AI startups can't deploy reliably to one server. We deploy to three in parallel.

05

69 Autonomous Sentinel Monitors

A tick-based pulse system runs 69 health checks at 4 cadences (5-minute, hourly, 6-hour, daily) — covering everything from Neo4j drift to NATS connectivity to security posture to prediction market accuracy. Replaces what would normally be 30+ separate monitoring services with a single ultra-lightweight scheduler. Problems are detected before anyone notices them.

06

Durable Work Queue with Dead Letter Recovery

Every task flows through JetStream-backed queues with 5-retry escalation and 30-day dead letter retention. Failed work doesn't vanish — it's preserved, replayable, and auditable. Supports 14+ task types: embeddings, gardening, corroboration discovery, website generation, fact extraction, call processing, cursor agent dispatch, and more.

07

Autonomous Website Generation

Describe what you want. MERIDIAN generates the site, pushes to GitHub, deploys to Render, and hands you a live URL. End to end, no human in the loop. Runs on M1 MacBook via background worker.

08

Multi-Daemon Collaboration & Deliberation

When a decision needs more than one perspective, MERIDIAN spins up a board of AI personas — selects 3-5 relevant daemons, runs them in parallel, synthesizes their responses, and records the transcript. Not prompt chaining. Actual multi-agent deliberation with addressed routing and audit trails.

09

Meeting Coach with Native Apple Silicon Processing

Live meetings or uploaded recordings → speaker diarization (diart + pyannote) → transcription (MLX Whisper, zero cloud cost) → pattern classification → per-participant coaching recommendations. Runs entirely on-device. Your call intelligence never leaves your hardware.

10

Research Pipeline with Domain Intelligence

Automated ingestion from documents → chunking → embedding → domain classification across 6 research domains → coherence-gated storage. Transform and consume are architecturally separated — the pipeline can't write junk to the graph. Every research artifact goes through the same promotion gates as manually created memories.

11

Temporal Decay & Evergreen Memory

Memories aren't treated equally forever. Non-critical memories lose influence over time via exponential decay (configurable half-life). Foundational knowledge (importance ≥ 9) is evergreen — exempt from decay entirely. This means the system naturally forgets noise while preserving what matters. No other system applies information theory to its own memory.

12

Security by Architecture

Write validation gates on every Neo4j mutation. Circuit breakers that auto-open after 5 failures. Identity lock with CAS semantics preventing duplicate daemon claims. Pre-commit hooks scanning for leaked secrets. GPG-encrypted credential store. TLS on all database connections. This isn't bolted-on security — it's structural.

13

Cursor Agent Parallel Offload

Mechanical tasks get dispatched to parallel cursor agent sessions running on isolated worktrees — 55+ pre-written task prompts with goal, constraints, acceptance criteria, and test commands. The main system focuses on judgment-heavy work. Routine work runs in parallel at machine speed. Human gets a Telegram ping when it's done.

14

Natural Language Mesh Access via Telegram

Talk to the mesh like you'd text a colleague. @mention specific daemons for their expertise. Multi-daemon group chat with transcript persistence. Alert bridge for real-time notifications. The entire system is accessible from your phone.
